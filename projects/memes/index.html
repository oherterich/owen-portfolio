The Meme Machine

The Meme Machine is a data visualization of the trends of memes over time. Eighteen diverse memes were selected and mapped over time. The results are not their overall popularity, but their trends: that is, the size of each meme over time is relative to the point in time in which it was most popular.

These memes were visualized from January 2011 until May 2013. Use the slider to see the changes over time, or select a meme to learn more and see its individual popularity graph.

Data for the trends comes from Google Trends. Memes were taken from Know Your Meme and the various meme images from around the web.
