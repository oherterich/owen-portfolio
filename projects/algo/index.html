Algorithmic Animation

A collection of work created for the Algorithmic Animation course at Parsons in Fall 2013. The course went over various aspects of animation using code, including trigonometry, particle systems, physics simulations, and motion.

All of the work was created with openFrameworks and a collection of add-ons. Thanks to Charlie Whitney for teaching the course.
