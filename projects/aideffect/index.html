Aid Effect

In this project, my group and I created a data visualization of the amount of aid given by the United States to a select group of countries. By comparing both economic and military aid with various data from other countries, such as death rate, we can see various correlations that might exist because of the effect of United States' aid.

Most of the data for this project was taken from the U.S. Census: U.S. Census Data
