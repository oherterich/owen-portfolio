About

Born on a dark winter's eve in the fruitful year of 1990, Owen soon emerged from youth with a vigorous passion for design, code, and bad rap music. He has a yearnin' for learnin', and as a result spends most of his time wasting away on the internet. Owen is an avid follower of Swansonism, a school of thought that focuses primarily on the relationship between man and bacon.

More prominently, Owen is interested in using technology to create engaging, thoughtful interactions in both digital and physical spaces.

Owen is currently attending Parsons The New School for Design in New York, where he is in pursuit of his MFA in Design & Technology.
